Jobs

Beekeeper

Assistant Bee-Keepers make sure that the bees are properly cared for. Employees will go out into the 40 properties where the bees are kept in order to make sure that the hives are healthy and are prepared for any potential threats. You must be able to regularly lift heavy objects. If you are allergic to bees, please consider applying to a different poistion.

Bottling Technician

Bottling technicians take the honey brought in by the bee-keepers and handle it from there. This includes processing the harvested honey to go into storage, bottling the honey as orders come in, and labeling the containers that the honey is sold in. These employees will also have frequent interactions with other tasks around the company such as pollen prodution, candle making, hive box builiding and painting, as well as hive set up.
