OUR BEES

How We Sell:

Nucs (5-frame nucleus colonies): each contains 5 frames of comb with honey, pollen, brood in all stages, bees and a young, laying queen in a convenient box for transportation. These boxes are great for aspiring beekeepers looking to "wet their feet" in the field!

Singles (10-frame boxes): each include 8 frames of comb with honey, pollen, brood in all stages, bees, a young & laying queen, feeder, bottom board, and cover.
